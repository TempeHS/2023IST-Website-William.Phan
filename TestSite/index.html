Hai Di Lao Instant Hotpot

Instant hotpot was created by Hai Di Lao Brand in 2022, with self-heating packets.

The Instant Hot-pot packets come in 2 flavours (tomato, and spicy), with up to 6 varieties of ingredients (beef, pork, vermicelli, vegan, lotus root, spicy chicken).
